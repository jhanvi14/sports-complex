Sportify Sports Complex

Sports Department was incepted in 1984. Earlier it was functioning under the umbrella of Education department Chandigarh.

The Department has established its full fledge establishments of Sports Directorate, District Sports Officer and Chandigarh Sports Council.

Director Sports is holding the charge of Sports Department under the supervision of the Secretary Finance and Sports, Chandigarh Administration.

Sportify Sports Complex has produced so many National & International players and athletes who have donned India colour in various disciplines of sports especially Hockey, Cricket Athletics, judo, Table Tennis, Weightlifting, Boxing, Wrestling and Skating etc.

About Us


Being Sports Enthusiast, The main aim behind this idea is to make all the sports facilities available to all the sportspeople under one roof.

       This facility will be made available to School & College students at affordable prices. We also welcome corporate customers.

             Our mission is to create awareness of sports & fitness amongst all people and thereby increasing positivity of mind.

To contribute towards producing top athletes who can achieve the highest targets in different competitive levels of sports to the Olympic level.
